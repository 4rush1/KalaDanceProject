Glossary
  • Guru: means teacher
  • Pandit: someone who is very knowledgeable in a certain area
  • Kala: means art
  • Tatkaar: the basis of all footwork, you tap your feet in the pattern right, left, right, left, left, right, left, right, rightâ€¦ and so on
  • tukda: pre-set footwork and hand movements
  • tihai: repeating a tukda 3 times
  • Chakar: spinning
  • Chakardaar tukda: pre-set footwork and hand movements which are repeated 3 times
  • Hastak: hand positions for dance, each hand position represents something
  • Gharana: a slight variation in dance style, originating from different regions
  • Ghungroo: bells tied to the feet
  • Natya Shastra: an ancient book about dance written around 200 to 500 BC

Classes


First class is free for all students!

  1. Junior Students: 3:30-5:30 pm on Mondays, Jennifer Evans Dance Studio, Samuel Marsden
  2. Runs for around 2 months and 1 week:
    • Learn how to count kathak beats (2 days)
    • Learn basic tatkaar (2 weeks)
    • Learn basic tukda (4 weeks)
    • Learn tihai (2 weeks)
    • Learn chakkar (1 week)
  1. Senior Students: 3:30-5:30 pm on Wednesdays, Jennifer Evans Dance Studio, Samuel Marsden
  2. Runs for around 2 months and 1 week:
    • Learn chakkardar tukdas (2 weeks)
    • Learn more complex tukdas (2 weeks)
    • Learn hastak (1 week)
    • Learn bhaav / emotions (1 week)
    • Choreograph a dance (4 weeks)

Equipment:


  • Comfortable loose clothing, e.g loose pants, churidar or salwar kameez
  • Ghungroo / bells that are tied to the feet
  • No footwear required while dancing
  • Notebook & pen
